<h2>Make sure you have access to the right version of Perl (5.8.x) or newer.</h2>

<code>
$ perl -v
</code>

<code>
$ perl -V
</code>

<code>
$ perl -e "print 42"
</code>

<code>
$ perl -E "say 42"
</code>

<h2>Check you can read the documentation. (perldoc)</h2>

<code>
$ perldoc perl
</code>

Press SPACE for next page and ENTER for next line.

Press q to quit.

<code>
$ perldoc perlsyn
</code>

<code>
$ aptitude search perl-doc
$ sudo aptitude install perl-doc
</code>
